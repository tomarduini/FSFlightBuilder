FS Flight Builder is a flight making application for FSX, FSXSE, and Prepar3D general aviation flights. Flight routes can be added manually or imported from the SkyVector Plain Text link. It automatically adds a briefing file to the aircraft kneeboard with departure and destination airport information, including runways, frequencies, weather, and charts.


The latest version is available at http://www.arduiniwebdevelopment.com/fsflightbuilder/fsflightbuilder.zip
